How to Start an AI Review Mining Service in 2026

After this step you will have: a clear offer, the core tools, and a practical plan to get your first result.

Back to All Ideas

Brands sit on thousands of customer reviews across Amazon, Etsy, Shopify, Google, and app stores, but most of that feedback never becomes action.
Input: one skill or interest and free AI tools
Output: one clear offer, sample deliverables, and a first client plan

What You Can Deliver

  • Top customer pain points and desired outcomes
  • Messaging angles pulled from real buyer language
  • Feature request summaries and complaint patterns
  • Competitor review comparisons
  • FAQ and product page suggestions based on review data

Tool Stack

How to Find Clients

Do this now:

  1. Target active Shopify brands, Amazon sellers, and SaaS products with visible reviews.
  2. Build a small sample insight report from public review data.
  3. Show how the insights could improve product pages or ad copy.
  4. Pitch it as faster customer research with direct business use.
Strong Angle

Real customer language often performs better than invented marketing copy. That is a clear, valuable outcome for clients.

Why This Business Works

It combines research, copy insight, and product strategy in one service. Once clients see usable insights, it becomes easy to expand into recurring research or adjacent strategy work.
